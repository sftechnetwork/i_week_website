What is International Week?


International Week has been celebrated at Ohio University as early as 1969, becoming an annual week-long celebration in 1979.

One of the most popular events of I Week is the International Street Fair, celebrated on Court Street since 1982 in conjunction with the City of Athens.

The International Student Union (ISU) funds, programs, and carries out many of the week's events and plays an integral role in event success. This year, I Week will be April 10-16, 2016.

We are looking forward to an exciting week of events that will engage and facilitate international awareness and diversity.
