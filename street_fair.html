What is the International Street Fair?

The International Street Fair has been a long standing tradition of Ohio University and the Athens community.

University student organizations and departments along with community organizations come together to celebrate our global diversity on Court Street. Organizations set up booths with cultural displays, free interactive cultural activities for kids, and food from around the world.

People come to enjoy the activities, watch the performances on the International Street Fair stage, and try the food during this global block party. This is a very family friendly event.

The location of the Street fair will be the Intersection of Court and Union, Uptown Athens


If you would like to participate in the International Street Fair, stop by Walter International Education Center, 15 Park Place to pick up an application packet March 16-March 21!
